Our 3-day Baking Process

PREPARATION

Sift together the flour and baking soda. Cream together the sugars, butter and salt with the puddle attachment, starting on low speed and increasing to medium speed, until light and fluffy. Scrape down the bowl as needed to blend evenly. Add the peanut butter and blend together. Add the sifted flour and baking soda and the cooled peanuts. Mix on low speed just long enough to combine the ingredients.

MAKING DOUGH

Heat the oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5. Cream the butter in a large bowl with a wooden spoon or in a stand mixer until it is soft. Add the sugar and keep beating until the mixture is light and fluffy. Sift in the flour and add the optional ingredients, if you’re using them. Bring the mixture together with your hands in a figure-of-eight motion until it forms a dough. You can freeze the dough at this point.

World Class Ingredients

PREMIUM FRENCH CHOCOLATE

Premium French chocolate is characterized by its high quality, refined flavors, and meticulous craftsmanship. It often features a higher cocoa content, resulting in a darker, less sweet, and more intense flavor profile. French chocolatiers are renowned for their expertise in selecting fine cocoa beans, using traditional techniques, and employing creative flavor combinations.

PRECISION MILLED-FLOUR

Precision milled flour refers to flour that is ground with great care and attention to detail, often on demand, to ensure optimal freshness and quality. This milling process often involves using specialized equipment like stone mills, which helps preserve the nutritional value and natural flavors of the grains.

SLOW-CHURNED BUTTER

Slow-churned butter is a type of butter made by slowly agitating cream, allowing the fat globules to clump together and form butter. This traditional method, often involving a maturation stage, develops a richer, creamier texture with a distinctive nutty or hazelnut flavor. The slow churning process allows the cream to develop its full aroma and sensory qualities.

TAHITIAN VANILLA BEANS

Tahitian vanilla beans, derived from the Vanilla tahitensis orchid, are known for their distinct floral, fruity, and slightly sweet flavor profile with notes of cherry, anise, and caramel. They are typically longer and plumper than other vanilla bean varieties, with a high oil content and a moist, supple texture. Their aroma is equally captivating, often described as sweet, cherry-like, and liquorish.

MALDON SEA SALT FLAKES

Maldon Sea Salt Flakes are renowned for their unique pyramid-shaped crystals and delicate texture, offering a clean, fresh flavor that enhances dishes without being overpowering. These flakes are known for their satisfying crunch and ability to dissolve quickly, making them ideal for finishing dishes or adding a subtle salty note.
